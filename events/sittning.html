Sittning

Sittning is a kind of dining event and is very common at universities in Sweden. Literaly translated to English as sitting it usually contains food, preformances and singing.

In EAA we usually arrange two big dinner parties every year for our members with 2 different themes, as is customary for a Sittning. “Sittning” is usallaly quite popular among our exchange student members as it allows them to experience something that is closer to traditional Swedish student life.
