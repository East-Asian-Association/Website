Christmas potlluck

Join EAA for a traditional Swedish Chirstmas dinner on Tuesday the 13th of December.

A longstanding Swedish tradition is celebrating christmas eve with a christmas dinner (or "Julbord" as we call it in Sweden) filled with christmas exclusive food.

The board will offer some traditional swedish food, but all our members are encouraged to bring traditional food from their home country!

Conversation Corner

Tuesdays and Thursdays from 17:15 - 18:15 we host Conversation Corner in the C-building at campus Valla.

From 18:15 we offer fika for those who want to stay and chat. Making friends with people from other countries is a great way to improve your language skills!

Tuesdays: Swedish and English
Thursdays: Japanese, Mandarin and Korean
