THE HEROIC TRIO | 東方三俠

Time: 23/5 18:00

By Johnnie To with Michelle Yeoh , Anita Mui , Maggie Cheung , Damian Lau, Anthony Wong 1992 | Hong Kong | 88 min | Cantonese - English subs

As newborn boys are kidnapped in the city, Tung, a superhero with incredible strength, teams up with Chat, a cunning bounty hunter, and Shadow, an enigmatic thief, to stop an evil eunuch from finding an heir who can dominate the world. The heroic trio races against time to save the innocent children and thwart the eunuch's plans, facing daunting challenges and unleashing thrilling fight scenes in an epic battle for the fate of the world.

AKIRA | アキラ

Time: 25/5 18:00

By Katsuhiro Ôtomo with the voices of Mitsuo Iwata, Nozomu Sasaki, Mami Koyama 1988 | Japan | 124 min | Japanese - English subs

After the world is devastated by the Great War, the decadent megacity Neo Tokyo is a place of constant unrest, plagued by criminal gangs, police brutality, corruption, and widespread unemployment. In this city of chaos, two young motorcyclists, Kaneda and Tetsuo, embark on a dangerous quest for the truth, risking everything to bring order to the city. But their search leads them down a path of dark secrets and deadly conspiracies that threaten to destroy them and the city they love. Akira is the anime and cyberpunk classic that redefined the genre and remains unparalleled in modern sci-fi history.

Conversation Corner

From 18:15 we offer fika for those who want to stay and chat. Making friends with people from other countries is a great way to improve your language skills!

Tuesdays: Swedish and English
Thursdays: Japanese, Mandarin and Korean
