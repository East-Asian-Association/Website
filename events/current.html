Conversation Corner

Tuesdays and Thursdays from 17:15 - 18:10 we host Conversation Corner in the C-building at campus Valla.

From 18:00 until later in the evening we offer fika for those who want to stay and chat. Making friends with people from other countries is a great way to improve your language skills!

Tuesdays: Swedish and English
Thursdays: Japanese, Mandarin and Korean
