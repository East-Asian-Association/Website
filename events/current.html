Christmas potlluck

Join EAA for a traditional Swedish Chirstmas dinner on Tuesday the 13th of December!

Celebrating christmas eve with a christmas dinner (or "Julbord" as we call it in Sweden) is a longstanding Swedish tradition. There are many Swedish foods exclusivly eaten during christmas that are hard to experience in other settings.

In addition of getting to experience Swedish food culuture, it is great a oportunity for our international members to share traditional food from their home countries as we encourage all our members to bring home made food to this event!

Conversation Corner

Tuesdays and Thursdays from 17:15 - 18:15 we host Conversation Corner in the C-building at campus Valla.

From 18:15 we offer fika for those who want to stay and chat. Making friends with people from other countries is a great way to improve your language skills!

Tuesdays: Swedish and English
Thursdays: Japanese, Mandarin and Korean
